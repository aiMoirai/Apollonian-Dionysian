Narratives

Choose a path to explore the collection following a clear and defined criterion. You can start by characters (Apollo / Dionysus) or by themes (performing arts, visual arts, literature).

Characters

Browse by figures and archetypes.

Apollo Dionysus
This narrative centers on Apollo and Dionysus as symbolic forces of power. The artworks explore how each deity embodies opposing principles, order, measure, and rational control versus disorder, ecstasy, and dissolution, revealing the tension between structure and chaos at the core of artistic creation.

Themes

Browse by artistic fields.

Performing arts Visual arts Literature
This narrative focuses on recurring thematic frameworks that cut across different artworks. By grouping works according to performing arts, visual arts, and literature, it highlights how Apollonian and Dionysian principles emerge, interact, and evolve across artistic languages.
