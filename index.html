TYRSOS & LYRE

Hello, visitor.
Take a glass of wine, the Symposium is about to start.

“Every artist is an 'imitator' when faced with such immediate artistic states of nature; that is to say, they are either an Apollonian artist of dream, or a Dyonisian artist of intoxication, or, finally— as in the case of Greek tragedy— an artist of both dream and intoxication.”
F. Nietzsche, The Birth of Tragedy.

Between light and shadow, order and ecstasy, the human spirit dances. Here, where the Apollonian meets the Dionysian, reason bows to rapture and form dissolves into frenzy.
Within this museum exhibition, you are invited to explore these forces in motion: wander freely through the galleries, follow the thematic paths that trace their dialogue across time, or delve into the full collection at your own pace.
Each way of visiting reveals a different melody in the ancient tension between harmony and passion, waiting for you to step closer and let it awaken your senses.
